The Home Selling Process

    Step 1: Prepare Your Home for Sale
  • Declutter, deep clean, and consider staging.
  • Complete necessary repairs or updates.
  • Enhance curb appeal to create a great first impression.
      • Step 2: Set the Right Price
  • Review comparable sales in your area.
  • Discuss a pricing strategy with your real estate agent.
  • Consider timing and current market conditions.
      • Step 3: Market the Property
  • Professional photography and listing description.
  • Promote through MLS, online platforms, and open houses.
  • Highlight key features and recent upgrades.
      • Step 4: Showings & Offers
  • Keep your home clean and ready for showings.
  • Review offers and negotiate terms.
  • Choose the best offer based on price and conditions.
      • Step 5: Under Contract & Escrow
  • Buyer completes inspection and appraisal.
  • Respond to any repair requests or contingencies.
  • Coordinate with title, lender, and agent throughout escrow.
      • Step 6: Closing
  • Sign final paperwork and transfer ownership.
  • Hand over the keys to the new owners.
  • Celebrate a successful home sale!
